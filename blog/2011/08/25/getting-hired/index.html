Getting Hired Like a Boss (Draft, Updates Coming)

Aug 25th, 2011

The last two weeks have been super exciting for me so I’d love to share my experience with you. But first of all if you haven’t gotten yourself a GitHub account yet – do yourself a favour and get it right now. You’ll thank me later, or you can send me 10% of your yearly salary once you get hired by one of the best companies in the Bay area (50% off standard recruiters’ fee). Got it? Good, continue to the cave of wonders… Another favour you can do yourself is grab a ticket to San Francisco and just come down here for a week or two right away. Stay at your friend’s place or use Airbnb. Again, thank me later – you’ll meet enormous amount of interesting and smart people and you’ll learn a lot. Chances are you’ll get an idea for your next startup here too, the atmosphere is just so refreshing here. A few G+ Photo Albums of the recent tech events:

  • HAPIHACK, event website here
