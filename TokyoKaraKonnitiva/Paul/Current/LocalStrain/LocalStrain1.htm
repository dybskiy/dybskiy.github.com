An approach for prediction of the fatigue crack initiation in airframe metal structures

Main features of the approach

A) Local strain concept  B) FE modeling 
  • Energy based criterion for fatigue damage accumulation
  • Residual strain amplitude as the criterion parameter 
  • An algorithm to build up stress-strain history in a notch tip
    •
  • Sub-modeling procedure for global-local hierarchical FE analysis of complex structure with concentrations
  • Linear material behavior within FE analysis
  • Contact problem for stress concentration around a fastener
    •


    Cases of study

    • B737 fuselage large-scale barrel specimen fatigue examination 

    • B747SR pressure bulkhead failure in 1985 

    • Wing panels design modification
